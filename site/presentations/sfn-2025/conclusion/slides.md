## And there's more!

<div class='column' style="float:left;width:50%;margin-top:10%">

Pynapple:

- `randomize`: shuffle timestamps to create surrogate datasets
- `spectrum`: compute power spectral densities
- `wavelet`: compute wavelet decompositions
- `warping`: linearly warp trial-based tensors

</div>

<div class='column' style="float:right;width:50%;margin-top:10%">
NeMoS:

- `Observation models`:
  - `GammaObservations`: calcium imaging
  - `BernoulliObservations`: behavioral data
  - `NegativeBinomialObservations`: over-dispersed spiking data
- `Regularizers`:
  - `GroupLasso`: apply Lasso (L1) regularization group-wise
  - `ElasticNet`: combine L1 and L2 regularization

</div>
